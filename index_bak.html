During this course I learned what an information system is: "A set of human, material and immaterial resources whose role is to collect, store, process and disseminate information within society." [1] YouTube is an example of IS. The first part of this course also sees the concept of business application (a complete application that can be used to handle several tasks specific to a field of activity). Informations from an IS can be stored both in the cloud and on an enterprise server. The information system improves the productivity of the company and promotes collaboration within it.

The second part of this course is about the Information Systems Department. We see the study pole (IS consultants + developers) and its role : to design and develop the information system. We also learn that the role of the RSSI (Responsible for the security of information systems) is to define the security policy of the IS. The project manager must exercise his prerogatives at all levels of the project, for example at the technical and organizational levels.

The third part concerns the challenges of the world of information systems. The complexity of IS has consequences : financial and human resource impacts as well as "the heterogeneity of applications, technologies and databases." [2] Several important concepts are seen : urbanization, technical architecture, IaaS, PaaS, SaaS, cloud computing and PRA (disaster recovery plan).

Finally, the last part was about the challenges of the IS world. Data and infrastructure are two security issues in the cloud. The course also talk about "cybernetic hygiene" ie an awareness of users of information systems about security.
